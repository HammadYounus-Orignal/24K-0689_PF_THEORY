Input:


- NUM1
- NUM2 


Processing:


1. Initialize TEMP to 0
2. Perform the Euclidean algorithm to find the GCD of NUM1 and NUM2
    - While NUM2 is not 0, swap NUM1 and NUM2, and update NUM1 to the remainder of NUM1 divided by NUM2
3. Determine if the GCD (NUM1) is 1


Output:


- "COPRIME" if the GCD is 1
- "NOT COPRIME" otherwise
